Monster Game — Summary Rules

Conversion Loop (Who Converts Who?)

🧛 Vampire → 🧍 Human → 👻 Ghost → 👽 Alien → 🤖 Robot → 🧟 Zombie → (repeat)

  • If your attack succeeds, your target becomes your species.
  • If your attack fails, you become theirs.

Special Rules & Exceptions

SpeciesSpecial Conversion Rules
🎯 Predator Kills Humans or Undead; the victim becomes Alien or Robot. Predator does not change.
🌕 Werewolf Acts as Human or Vampire depending on the round; follows that current form for conversions.
🌀 Shapeshifter On success, converts into any simple species of choice. On fail, becomes the target’s species.
⚡ Transformer Converts any simple species to Aliens (except Humans and Ghosts). Counts as Alien for victory. All Specialists convert Transformers → Ghosts.
👻 Ghost Converts Aliens. Can Haunt (blocks attacks). If a Human or Vampire attacks a haunted target, the haunt is lifted without conversion. Haunts last until the end of the round and do not transfer on conversion.

Game Rules

Game Duration: 4 rounds (≈10 min each)

Objective: Win as your current species by end of Round 4

Attacking

  • Tap on shoulder or wink (must be seen)
  • Say a casual sentence including your species’ keyword
  • Success: target converts to your species
  • Fail: you convert to their species
  • Cannot attack the same person twice in a row

Haunting (Ghosts only)

  • 1 haunt per round (target cannot attack)
  • If a Human or Vampire attacks a haunted target → the haunt is lifted without conversion
  • Haunts last until the end of the round and do not transfer upon conversion

Conversion Notes

  • When converted: follow your new species’ rules immediately
  • Transformers count as Aliens for winning
  • Werewolves + Shapeshifters win with final species
